About Us

We are a up and coming organization that belives in the spirit of the union and that one can help the other. As a result, We have come up with SAT Decoded, an open source repository of information for a student who has just began thier preparation for the SAT exam. As a result, Those who have done the SAT can contribute to the website by uploading videos and difficult questions that they found while they practiced for the SAT, which will help the next batch of students benefit more and more each year.
